# Investor Demo Script (~3 minutes)

## Setup
- Open http://localhost:3000 in Chrome
- Have the demo PDF ready on desktop
- Close all other tabs and notifications

## Flow

### 1. The Problem (30 sec — YOU talk, don't show)
"Estimators spend 6-8 hours manually measuring construction drawings. Our platform uses AI to do this in seconds."

### 2. Upload (15 sec)
- Drag the PDF onto the upload zone
- "Watch — I drop a 50-page MEP set and the AI takes over."

### 3. AI Processing (7 sec)
- Don't talk over this. Let the staged progress labels speak.
- At most: "It's extracting pages, running OCR, and detecting components."

### 4. The Reveal (60 sec)
- Polygons appear. Pause for 1 second. Let it land.
- "30 components detected automatically. Rooms, corridors, mechanical equipment."
- Zoom into a room. "Each detection has its area, perimeter, and a confidence score."
- Click a fixture. "We classified every light fixture, VAV box, and fire panel."

### 5. Review Workflow (45 sec)
- Accept a few polygons: "The estimator reviews each detection. Accept, reject, or delete."
- Press keyboard 'a' to accept rapidly: "Keyboard shortcuts for fast review."
- Click "Accept All Pending": "Or bulk-accept and fix the exceptions."
- "This turns 6 hours into 10 minutes."

### 6. Export (15 sec)
- Click Export CSV
- Open the downloaded CSV briefly
- "Clean CSV ready to paste into their bid spreadsheet. Done."

### 7. Close
- "Model generalization across plan types is our next milestone. We're raising to train on commercial MEP plans and onboard our first pilot customers."

## Emergency Fallbacks
- If upload fails: Cmd+Shift+D to force-load the prebaked demo
- If viewer fails: have the Loom recording ready
- If asked "can I try my own PDF?": "We'd love to run your plans — let's set that up after the call."
- Logo tapped 5 times rapidly: resets the demo to a clean state
